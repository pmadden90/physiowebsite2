Pilates

Physiotherapy led Pilates is an excellent way of improving your strength, balance and overall conditioning in a safe and controlled environment. Before attending the class, each participant is required to attend a pre-assessment. This is an hour long appointment which involves the Physiotherapist gathering information on your medical history, and then teaching you about the basics of what Pilates involves. The class numbers are small to ensure each person gets the attention they require throughout each hour long class.
